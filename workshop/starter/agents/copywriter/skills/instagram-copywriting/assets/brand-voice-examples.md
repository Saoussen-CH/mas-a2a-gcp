# Brand Voice Reference Examples

These are annotated caption examples. Use them to calibrate tone before writing for a new brand.

---

## Example 1 - Wellness / Clean beauty (Inspirational tone)

```
Your skin doesn't need more products. It needs the right ones.

We spent 2 years formulating a moisturizer that does the work of five.
No fragrance. No filler. Just the 6 ingredients your skin barrier
actually recognizes.

This is what "less is more" looks like in a bottle.

.
#cleanbeauty #minimalistskincare #skincareingredients #glowingskin
#nontoxicbeauty #skinbarrier #skincareroutine
CTA: Save this and check our bio link for the full ingredient breakdown.
```

**What works:** Contrarian hook, specific proof point (2 years, 6 ingredients), no product name push.

---

## Example 2 - Fitness / Supplement (Educational tone)

```
You're not tired because you didn't sleep enough.

You're tired because your cells aren't producing energy efficiently.

Mitochondria need magnesium, B vitamins, and CoQ10 to convert food
into fuel. Most people are low in all three.

That's the energy gap nobody talks about.

.
#energylevels #mitochondria #supplementscience #magnesium
#biohacking #functionalhealth #fatigue
CTA: Send this to someone who's always exhausted.
```

**What works:** Reframes a common problem, uses science without being inaccessible, CTA is shareable.

## Example 3 - Sustainable lifestyle (Community tone)

```
We're not perfect. And we're done pretending anyone is.

Sustainable living isn't about zero-waste. It's about choosing
better when you can, without the guilt when you can't.

You're already doing more than you think.

What's one small swap you've made this year? We genuinely want to know.

.
#sustainableliving #ecofriendly #zerowaste #sustainablelifestyle
#greenliving #climateaction #slowliving
CTA: Tell us in the comments - every swap counts.
```

**What works:** Anti-perfectionism stance creates belonging, question is open-ended not yes/no.

## Example 4 - DTC food / snack (Story-driven tone)

```
I used to hit a wall at 3pm every day.

Reached for coffee. Then another coffee. Then wondered why
I couldn't sleep.

Switched to a 20g protein snack at 2:45. The wall disappeared.

Turns out the crash wasn't caffeine deficiency. It was protein.

.
#proteinsnacks #afternoonslump #nutritionscience #highprotein
#healthysnacking #bloodsugar #mealprep
CTA: Save this for your next grocery run.
```

**What works:** Personal story structure, specific time details add credibility, insight-led CTA.
