# Caption Formulas

## Hook formulas (first line)

Use exactly one of these patterns per caption. The hook must be complete before the fold.

| Formula | Example |
|---------|---------|
| **Contrarian claim** | "Drinking more water won't fix your energy crash." |
| **Specific number** | "3 things your morning routine is missing." |
| **Direct address** | "If your skin is dry by noon, read this." |
| **The secret** | "The reason your sunscreen isn't working isn't the SPF." |
| **Story opening** | "I almost quit. Then I changed one thing." |
| **Bold promise** | "This saved me 40 minutes every morning." |
| **Question that stings** | "When did you last drink water that wasn't coffee?" |

## CTA formulas

Match the CTA to the caption tone:

| Tone | CTA pattern | Example |
|------|-------------|---------|
| Inspirational | Save + future action | "Save this for the morning you need a reset." |
| Educational | Share for value | "Send this to a friend who needs to hear it." |
| Community | Invite answer | "Tell us in the comments - morning or night person?" |
| Urgency | Immediate action | "Tap the link in bio before this sells out." |
| Story | Relate + follow | "Follow for more real talk about [topic]." |

## Full caption structures

### Structure A - Problem/Solution (Educational/Inspirational)

```
[Hook - contrarian claim or specific number]

Here's what most people get wrong:
[2-3 short lines expanding the problem]

The fix:
[2-3 lines of the solution]

[Optional: 1-line brand tie-in, not salesy]

.
[Hashtags]
CTA: [Save / share prompt]
```

### Structure B - Story Arc (Story-driven)

```
[Hook - mid-scene opening]

[2-3 lines: the struggle or turning point]

[2-3 lines: the insight or outcome]

[1 line: the universal truth this unlocks]

.
[Hashtags]
CTA: [Relate + follow prompt]
```

### Structure C - List / Carousel Tease (Educational)

```
[Hook - specific number]

[Item 1]
[Item 2]
[Item 3]
[Item 4 - save the best for last or a carousel]

Which one surprised you most? Drop it below.

.
[Hashtags]
CTA: [Comment prompt]
```

### Structure D - Urgency / Social Proof

```
[Hook - bold claim or social proof stat]

[1-2 lines: the FOMO trigger]

[1-2 lines: the benefit they're missing]

[1 line: soft scarcity]

.
[Hashtags]
CTA: [Direct action - link in bio, DM, limited offer]
```

### Structure E - Community / Belonging

```
[Hook - direct address to a specific identity]

[2-3 lines: "we" language, shared experience]

[1 line: brand as part of their tribe, not as a vendor]

[Open-ended question]

.
[Hashtags]
CTA: [Conversation starter in comments]
```
